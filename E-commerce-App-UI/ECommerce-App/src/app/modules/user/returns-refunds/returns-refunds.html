Return Policy
Items can be returned within 7 days of delivery
Product must be unused and in original packaging
All tags, labels, and accessories must be intact
Product should be in the same condition as received
How to Return
1

Go to My Orders

Open My Orders and select the order you want to return

2

Select Return Reason

Choose the reason for return and provide any additional details

3

Schedule Pickup

Our delivery partner will pick up the item from your address

4

Refund Processed

After inspection, refund is credited to your original payment method
