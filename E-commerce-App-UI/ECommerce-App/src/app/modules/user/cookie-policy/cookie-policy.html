This Cookie Policy explains how Buyverse uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our platform. It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control their use.

1

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and provide information to site owners.

Cookies can be "persistent" (remain on your device until deleted) or "session" cookies (deleted when you close your browser).

2

  • Security: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity and unauthorized access

5

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block all or specific cookies
  • Set preferences for certain websites
  • Get notified when a cookie is being set

Disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using key features like logging in, adding items to cart, and placing orders.
